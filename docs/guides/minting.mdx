# Token Minting

## How It Works

Minting creates a complete set of outcome tokens for a market by spending simulated USDT.

### Binary Markets

Spend **1 USDT** to receive:
- 1 **Yes** token
- 1 **No** token

### Multi-Outcome Markets

Spend **1 USDT** to receive:
- 1 token for **each** outcome

## Why Mint?

Minting is the primary way to create liquidity in a market. After minting, you can:

1. **Sell tokens you disagree with** — If you think "Yes" will win, sell your "No" tokens
2. **Provide liquidity** — Place orders on both sides of the book
3. **Arbitrage** — If token prices sum to more than 1.00, mint and sell for profit

## Settlement

When a market resolves:
- **Winning tokens** → Redeemed for 1.00 USDT each
- **Losing tokens** → Worth 0.00 USDT
- **Open orders** → Automatically cancelled, locked funds released
